FAQs

Ask your doubts.

  • What is Spark?

    SPARK is a one-month long event organized by AIT OSS club, exclusively for the first-year engineering students to introduce them to the field of web development.

  • What are the benefits?

    You will have a chance to kickstart your web development journey completely free of cost.
    You'll have a chance to learn and spread awareness about different open source technologies.
    You'll have a chance to learn from experienced mentors, interact with industry professionals and win exciting prizes.

  • Is there any mentor support?

    24/7 Mentor guidance will be there, you can reach out to them through Social Media handels provided in mentors section.

  • Do we have to pay?

    No, it's completely free of cost.
